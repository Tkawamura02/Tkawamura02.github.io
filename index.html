About Me

"Never, ever ever ever ever give up."

- Winston Churchill

When I was younger, I would sneak into my parents room to look at their bookshelves. They always seemed like towering buildings that were ready to topple any second and when I found anything of interest I would use chairs to reach the higher shelves. On this rather peculiar day I snooped around my father's desk, which was always meticulously clean as he was cleaning freak. There was one thing that stood out to my childish eyes: a bookmark on the floor. "What does it say?" I thought to myself while reaching for it. There was nothing special about it. In fact, it was rather plain. "Never, ever ever ever ever give up" it read. "Well that's odd" I said outloud.



Dear reader,

You must be wondering why I wrote about this particular event. I have my reasons which I will write to you henceforth. I continued about my childish hobbies without a worry on my mind. But as I grew older, it stuck with me. It forever changed the way I approached problems and thought about conflicts.

Yours faithfully,

Tiffany Kawamura



I was never one to meddle with other people's business but I later brought it up with my father fifteen years later. I questioned him and asked why he even kept an old bookmark like that. He told me that that was his favorite quote when he was younger.
